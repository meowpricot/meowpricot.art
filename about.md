### preramble
hello again! if you're here i suppose you want to know more about me!! as stated on the index, i'm *em*, the webkitty of [meowpricot.art](/)! my hobbies are art, crafts and gaming - but i'm sure you want to know more about those hobbies - so, to ramble:
#### art
art is absolutely everything to me. it's my lifeblood, it's my soul in tangible form. it just makes me happy, i love getting an idea or inspiration and just. making something with it! it's so exciting going into a piece and expressing myself through it! even though i'm primarily a digital artist, i really enjoy experimenting with watercolours and pencils.
#### crafts
ok crafts is really vague, so i'll be more specific with what i like to craft! i'm currently super into making kandi bracelets! i hope to show them off on this site someday! i'm also kinda into sewing, but it's been really difficult toactually *make* anything. there's no fabric stores in town, so i'm working with old bedsheets from op shops... but the few things i have made, made me really happy, so i'd like to do more! i also journal, if that counts as a craft? i like sticker-bombing, it's another art form for me.
#### gaming
unsurpisngly, the person who is nerdy enough to code a website, is nerdy enough to play video games. like almost everyone into video games, i started gaming when i was super little! i couldn't tell you what my first video game was, as i probably just pried the controller from my parents' hands... but the first game i really had a 'connection' with was spyro! i'm not sure if i played spyro 2 first or spyro a hero's tale first (both were on the ps2, and played around the same time) but that seires as a whole really made me a Gamer. nowadays, the main game i really play now is wizard101. i also play a little bit of warframe! i used to be super into FFXIV, but dawntrail's post-MSQ content hasn't been enough to keep me interested. maybe i'll get into it again someday!
