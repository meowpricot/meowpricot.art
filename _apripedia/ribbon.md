<b>TRIGGER WARNING: blood & gore, especially in the gallery</b>
<br>ribbon is a crimstain - what could be equated to a vampire in our universe. they are one of the founding members of fortune offices, a mercenary-for-hire group. ribbon is the partner, and almost pet, of francell, another member of fortune offices.
<br><br>ribbon is known as the "usurper" to their family of crimstain. essentially, it befalls upon them to kill the "progenitor" of the family - the person who rules and controls the crimstain of that family. however, this progenitor happens to be ribbon's father!

<br>ribbon is a character for the tabletop game <em>apotheosis</em> - a rulebook one of my friends made!

### appearance

ribbon is a short and somewhat stout humanoid with porcelain white skin. they have short, unkempt white hair that frames their face. their eyelashes match their hair, white and long. they have a pair of bright crimson eyes, with slit pupils.
<br><br>
they typically wear outfits adorned with red ribbons. in their main outfit, they wear a grey cropped coat with white ruffles lining the bottom of the torso. on top of their coat is a dark red holster with heart adornments. on their lower half is a dark red skirt, with a white petticoat, and a pair of dark red boots with red ribbons. on their head is an oversized dark red beret with a red ribbon on the back.
<br><br>
their main weapon, <em>heartbreaker</em>, is a grey greatsword about the same height as ribbon themselves. on the tip of the handle is a red heart, and wrapped around that handle is a HUGE red ribbon.
<br><br>
<b>when in their remembrance,</b> ribbon adopts the persona of "rosalinde". their outfit transforms into a dark red lolita dress, adorned with ruffles and ribbons. they lose the beret, and now walk barefooted. their weapon turns into the <em>seolfor cruciform</em>, a silver crucifix embedded into their chest.

### personality

ribbon is an excitable crimstain with a large appetite for... a lot of things. like pudding, human blood, combat, and their partner, francell! they are always bouncing around and getting into trouble, often acting before thinking. this gets them into trouble often.

<br><br>despite their aptitude for Not Thinking Much, ribbon is an extremely keen person when it comes to deciphering how someone actually feels. they can quickly tell if someone is hiding something, or if they're acting coy - but ribbon will not sugar coat how that makes them feel, either.
<br><br>
<b>when in their remembrance,</b> rosalinde lets go of all inhibitions and becomes feral. their speech goes from silly - yet controlled, to completely wild and loud. their only thought is more blood and more to feast upon!
