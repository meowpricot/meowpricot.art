biscuit is <a href="/about">meowpricot</a>'s main fursona. 
<br><br>
biscuit is an anthropomorphic cat who lives in a cottage somewhere in the woods. they primarily work as an artist, but they also enjoy tending to their flower garden, and making friends in the forest!
<br><br>
biscuit has a girlfriend, <a href="https://toyhou.se/23516900.jynx-m-k" target="_blank">jynx</a>. they want jynx to live with them in the woods, but jynx loves her arcade job just too much... so they take turns staying at each other's homes.
<br><br>
biscuit wears a mystical pendant on their neck at all times. it's said the pendant is actually magical...

### appearance

biscuit is an anthropomorphic cat with calico colouration. their face is white on the bottom half, and a split between a light pink on the right and a light orange on the left - on the upper half of their face. they have fluffy, short brown hair, with messy bangs across their forehead. on top of their head is a pair of cat ears, with a pink inside and a slight gradient (from orange -> pink -> white) on the tips.
<br><br>
their face can be described as round and cute, with big brown eyes, and a small pink nose. their mouth is almost always in a cute cat-like smile.
<br><br>
their body is similarly covered in a white fur, with their left-side limbs being a light orange, and their right-side limbs being a light pink. their paws are white, with pink paw pads. their body shape is plump and fat, with a larger lower-half.
<br><br>
they have a long, super fluffy tail. their tail is brown at the base, which then grows into a gradient of light orange into light pink, with a white tip.

### personality

biscuit can be described as chipper and excitable around friends, but quiet and polite around strangers and acquaintances. they tend to fidget and play with their paws while speaking, and they struggle to maintain eye contact. despite sometimes seeming like they don't enjoy talking - biscuit actually adores making new friends and hanging out with them too!
<br><br>they find it fun to give their friends lots of gifts! specifically, hand-made gifts, like personal sketches and doodles, or little bouquets, or even baked goods. they always make sure to include a hand-written letter, or a sticker or two, with all their gifts - they think it makes it even sweeter!

- biscuit has been my fursona since at least 2023!
- they were originally based on color point cats, and they still are a little, but i've since changed their fur pattern to pay homage to my childhood cats cinder, and pebbles, who have both since passed away. cinder was an orange tabby, and pebbles, her daughter, was a calico!
