my wizards

Emma DreamDreamer


  • school: life
  • level: 180
  • currently: farming BiS in darkmoor
  • creation date: february 2015

emma dreamdreamer is a wizard that claims to be from marleybone. they are a highly skilled theurgist, known for their ability to heal almost anything! they are an avid gardener who finds joys more in hobbies than saving the world... alas, there's always a healer in need!

while i did have an early childhood account, i've since lost access to it (and honestly, there wasn't anything actually important on it). when i tried out the game again, i made emma, and i got completely hooked on it! i have so many happy memories on this wizard :)

(btw as a kid i thought "dreamdreamer" was hilarious)

Brynn StarCatcher


  • school: ice
  • level: 115
  • currently: questing in mirage
  • creation date: december 2017

a harsh survivalist from the bitter chill of polaris, brynn is on a quest to find out what happened to her sister, calamity. while she acts cold on the outside, brynn herself is actually quite kind-hearted. she travels with the rambunctious tiffany legendthief to discover the secrets of the spiral!

brynn was the first character i created to quest with my (now) girlfriend! we started questing on brynn (ice) and calamity (fire), but my girlfriend since swapped to a myth wizard instead (tiffany)! we thought it'd be fun to weave that into their storylines.

currently on semi-hiatus as we're questing on different wizards right now!

Grace DawnSong


  • school: storm
  • level: 84
  • currently: questing in azteca
  • creation date: unknown, likely 2019 ~ 2020

grace is a punky, rebellious storm wizard from wizard city. she came from a known family of powerful wizards, and grace herself is just as powerful as her bloodline. she's highly intelligent and cunning, and very self-assured, but secretly cares so much about her friends. she would stand up for them in a heartbeat!

grace, for the longest time, was kind of stuck at level 30 with nothing much to do. that was until my girlfriend decided "i wanna play a death wizard!" and that gave me the perfect opportunity to finally play grace! playing as a storm wizard is honestly so much fun, and trying to get the best stats for my level is really engaging!
