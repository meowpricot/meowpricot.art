The Story

The story of Wizard101 is such a fun journey that I always look forward to when I start a new Wizard. To keep things spoiler free, I will only lightly touch on the first arc's story!

The story begins in Wizard City, where you, known as the Wizard, arrive in Golem Court. You are introduced to Merle Ambrose, the headmaster of Ravenwood school of Magical Arts, and his friend, Gamma the owl. you're about to go through initiation - but oh no! suddenly, as a lightning strike hits the tower, you see an evil silhouette through the window... merle instructs you to enter the tower, and once inside, you come face to face with malistaire! he challenges you to a fight - but he wont fight you himself, no, he makes his evil minions fight you instead! (this is where the combat tutorial takes place!)

once you defeat his minions, malistaire flees, and you're left to finish your initation!

from this point, you slowly unravel the story behind the first arc - traevelling from wizard city, to the ancient krokotopia, the rooftops of marleybone, the nature of mooshu, and finally, to dragonspyre - where you face off against mailistaire!
