## A Wizard101 Web Shrine

hello and welcome to *A Theurgist's Letter to the Spiral*, a web shrine dedicated to Wizard101. Wizard101 is an MMO from 2008 developed and publised by Kingsisle Entertainment! in Wizard101, you get to create a Wizard, picking from one of seven Schools of Magic. with your Wizard, you must go on an adventure to save the Spiral, using spell cards you learn along the way!

<br>

Wizard101 is a cherished childhood game to me, and I hope to share just how much I love this game with this small web shrine. it's combat is unlike any other MMO I've played, and it's story ranges from whimsical and fun, to some of the most emotional writing I've ever had the pleasure of reading. *(by the way, all dialogue in Wizard101 is voiced!)*

<br>

I recommend everyone give Wizard101 a go. the free-to-play section lasts until the start of Krokotopia *(the second world)*, which should be enough for you to tell if you enjoy the combat or not. if you listen to the dialogue, that section will take a few hours!

## On the Title
*A Theurgist's Letter to the Spiral*. A Theurgist, more well known as a Life Wizard, is the School of my main character. I want this web shrine to feel like a love letter to the game of sorts - besides, that's basically what this is. Think of this as my own character writing back to home.
